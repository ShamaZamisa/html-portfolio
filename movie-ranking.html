The Best Movies according to Shama

My top 3 movies of all time.

Guess Who

I generally love comedy. It's a hilarious movie that documents an interacial couple falling in love and the racial tensions that ensue when the girl's family wants to meet the guy.

Shaolin Soccer

I love the fact that it is an overanimated, exaggeration of a movie. A break from reality.

Black Panther

A movie about the only African that eluded colonization by keeping its valuable natural resources a secret from the outside world.
